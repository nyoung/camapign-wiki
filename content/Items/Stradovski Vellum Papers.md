# Stradovski's Vellum Papers (4 sheets)

## Description
Four sheets of expensive engraved vellum — the kind that lasts forever and is worth a small fortune. Taken from a secret compartment in [[Professor Udo Stradovski]]'s desk drawer at the [[Collegium Theologica]].

## Qualities
- Extraordinarily durable and valuable
- Worth significant money

## Properties / Special Rules
The guard permitted them to take these discreetly after [[Hildegarde]] made an emotional appeal about saving [[Augustus Steiner]]'s life.

## Notes
- Hidden in a secret compartment — Stradovski went to unusual lengths to conceal these
- The guard noted hiding supplies is common among scholars due to theft — but a secret compartment is more than typical precaution
- Content of any writing on the vellum not yet examined
