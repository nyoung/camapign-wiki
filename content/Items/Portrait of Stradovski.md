# Portrait of Stradovski

## Description
A painted portrait of a dark-haired man, cut from its frame. Found at [[Professor Udo Stradovski]]'s house. On the back: "Udo Stradovski" and "2505" — the session notes place this approximately eighteen years ago.

## Properties / Special Rules
Useful for identifying the professor.

## Notes
- The portrait was cut from its frame — someone (possibly Stradovski himself) removed it deliberately; the frame was left behind
- The dark-haired description matches the militia warrant description of Stradovski
- The date "2505" noted on the back but session describes as "eighteen years ago" — may warrant clarification
