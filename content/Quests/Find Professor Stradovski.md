# Find Professor Stradovski

## Objective
Locate [[Professor Udo Stradovski]] and return him to [[Middenheim]] before the [[Ordo Sancti Inquisita]] finds and executes him — and before [[Augustus Steiner]]'s execution deadline (~6 weeks from 18 Nachexen 2512).

## Background
[[Professor Udo Stradovski]] was accused of blasphemy and fled [[Middenheim]], leaving his student [[Augustus Steiner]] imprisoned as his legal bond. Augustus faces execution if the professor is not produced. The party, the [[Ordo Sancti Inquisita]], and Ulrican White Wolves are all racing to find him. The professor is not merely a rogue academic — he is ruthless, has an active support network, and appears deeply enmeshed in forbidden disease research.

## Known Information
- Physical description: ~50 years old, dark hair, brown eyes, average height, own teeth; [[Portrait of Stradovski]] available
- Heresy charge: claimed gods don't exist, priests are merely magicians
- Known for miraculous cures — the [[Seuchenbuch|Seuchenbuch (Plague Book)]] discusses using disease as tool/weapon; Berin confirmed it resembles controversial texts held by Knights Panther
- Drugged [[Augustus Steiner]] with tea to secure bond and escape
- No Ulric shrine in Middenheim home — suspicious
- Subject of a medical negligence lawsuit (unverified rumour)
- Rumour of necrophilia with a dead student (unverified rumour)
- [[Stradovski Vellum Papers]] — expensive hidden documents; contents unknown
- **True destination: [[Ferlangen]]** (mining town, eastern Ostland) — Salzenmund was a deliberate false trail arranged by [[Elfrieda Teuschel]]
- **Moral character confirmed (Session 15):** Sacrificed his travelling companion [[Schliegel]] and a horse to the ghoul-master [[Melmoth]] at the [[Forest Inn]] in exchange for his own safe passage — also shared academic knowledge about ghoul creation/care. He is dangerous.
- Inquired about [[Seuchenshof]] hospice at [[Skralberg]] — road barricaded; proceeded east into [[Ostland]]
