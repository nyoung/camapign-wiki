# Joerg Wusterburg

> **Note:** Joerg was a PC who has since retired from the game. He is now treated as an NPC.

## Description
An Ulrican priest — specifically a novice of the [[Order of the Howling Wolf]]. Met the party over a communal meal at the [[Temple of Shallya]].

## Role / Occupation
Novice of the [[Order of the Howling Wolf]] (Ulrican). Also enrolled in the [[Middenland Militia]] as a provisional member alongside the party.

## Personality
Visibly nervous and flustered about the Stradovski retrieval task.

## Relationships
- [[Order of the Howling Wolf]] — his order
- [[Middenland Militia]] — provisional member
- Party — fellow contractor on the [[Find Professor Stradovski]] quest

## History with the Party
- [[Session 02 - The Trial of Klaus Grebel]]: Met at the [[Temple of Shallya]] over dinner.
- [[Session 03 - The Trial]]: Registered alongside the party for the militia warrant; identified himself as a novice of the Order of the Howling Wolf.
- [[Session 06 - The Were-Rabbit]] / [[Session 07 - The Were-Rabbit's End]]: Fought in the were-rabbit battle at [[Fox and Crown]]; wounded — leg slashed and knocked back by the creature, then further bitten by rabbit swarms, left badly wounded. After the battle, announced his intention to return to [[Middenheim]] to report the chaos beast to the [[Order of the Howling Wolf]] temple. **Departed the party on 22 Nachexen 2512.** Hildegarde encouraged him to keep in touch.

## Known Information
- Novice of the [[Order of the Howling Wolf]]
- Has agreed to help find [[Professor Udo Stradovski]]
- Enrolled as provisional [[Middenland Militia]] member (pay: 8 or 10 shillings/day)
