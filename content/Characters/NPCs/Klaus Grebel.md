# Klaus Grebel

## Description
Young man. Former Shallyan novitiate turned radical street speaker. Elke Grebel's brother.

## Role / Occupation
Street agitator. Delivers speeches about structural oppression by merchant classes and guilds. Now serving community service under the [[Cult of Shallya]].

## Personality
Passionate, provocative. Invoked Sigmar's name in his rhetoric — the judge interpreted this as rhetorical (using Sigmar's humble origins against guild cartels) rather than doctrinal heresy.

## Relationships
- [[Elke Grebel]] — sister
- [[Danderich Flisshausten]] / [[Ordo Sancti Inquisita]] — arrested and prosecuted him
- [[Pilpoul Ivri|Pilpoul]] — pointed him out to the Inquisitor in Session 01; then defended him in court in Session 03
- [[Udo Hintzer|Watch Sergeant Udo Hintzer]] — took him from the Inquisitor's custody to jail for a proper trial
- [[Konrad Stellmann]] — volunteer defence counsel

## History with the Party
- [[Session 01 - The Sewer Horror]]: Encountered during the plaza riot. [[Pilpoul Ivri|Pilpoul]] identified him to the [[Danderich Flisshausten]]. Arrested.
- [[Session 02 - The Trial of Klaus Grebel]]: Surrendered to the Inquisitor, but [[Udo Hintzer|Watch Sergeant Udo Hintzer]] intervened and took him to jail. Trial scheduled at [[Fulpfasser Court]].
- [[Session 03 - The Trial]]: Found **guilty** of the Heresy of Arctus but sentenced to **3 months minimum community service** under the [[Cult of Shallya]] — no execution, no long-term imprisonment. [[Pilpoul Ivri|Pilpoul]] effectively won a lenient sentence. Back at the church per [[Elke Grebel]]; the Inquisition cannot kill him.

## Known Information
