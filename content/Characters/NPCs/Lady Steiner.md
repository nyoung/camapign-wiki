# Lady Steiner

## Description
A distressed noblewoman. Introduced to the party by [[Matron Isolde]] at the [[Temple of Shallya]].

## Role / Occupation
Noblewoman. Seeking aid to rescue her son [[Augustus Steiner]] from execution.

## Personality
Distressed but composed enough to conduct business. Recognized [[Hildegarde]]'s brass necklace as "familiar" — a detail she was willing to leverage in exchange for information about Hildegarde's mother.

## Relationships
- [[Augustus Steiner]] — son, currently imprisoned in his mentor's place
- [[Professor Udo Stradovski]] — her son's mentor, whose disappearance caused this crisis
- [[Temple of Verena]] / Legalists — co-funding the retrieval job
- [[Matron Isolde]] — connected; brought her to meet the party
- [[Hildegarde]] — recognised her brass necklace; agreed to inquire about Hildegarde's missing mother in exchange for the necklace and the party taking the job

## History with the Party
Met in [[Session 02 - The Trial of Klaus Grebel]]. Offered an Imperial warrant and payment from the [[Temple of Verena]] and legalists for the retrieval of [[Professor Udo Stradovski]]. Accepted [[Hildegarde]]'s brass necklace in exchange for agreeing to investigate Hildegarde's missing mother.

## Known Information
- Her son [[Augustus Steiner]] faces execution in ~6 weeks unless the professor is found
- [[Augustus Steiner]] acted as legal bond for [[Professor Udo Stradovski]], and now sits in prison in his place
- Recognized [[Hildegarde]]'s brass necklace — claimed it was "familiar"
