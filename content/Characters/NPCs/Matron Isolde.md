# Matron Isolde

## Description
The matron of the [[Temple of Shallya]] in [[Middenheim]]. A person of evident authority within the temple.

## Role / Occupation
Matron — senior clergy of the [[Cult of Shallya]] at the Middenheim temple.

## Personality
Welcoming and pragmatic. Offered the party sanctuary, clean clothes, and food without hesitation. Trusted [[Elke Grebel]]'s account of the party's heroism and treated them accordingly.

## Relationships
- [[Elke Grebel]] — novice under her care
- [[Klaus Grebel]] — knows him as Elke's brother and a former Shallyan novitiate
- [[Lady Steiner]] — brought her to the party; clearly connected to the temple's broader interests
- Party — host and benefactor; gave them shelter and facilitated the Stradovski job

## History with the Party
Met in [[Session 02 - The Trial of Klaus Grebel]]. Welcomed the party, provided sanctuary and clean clothes for the night, and facilitated the introduction to [[Lady Steiner]].

## Known Information
- [[Klaus Grebel]] was formerly a Shallyan novitiate before radicalizing as a street speaker
- Is aware of the political situation around Klaus's arrest for "heresy"
- Has connections to [[Lady Steiner]] and by extension the [[Temple of Verena]] and legalists
