# Fya Vater

## Description
[[Professor Udo Stradovski]]'s former live-in maid. Now staying at the [[Pit Fight Tavern]] in the Alt-Quartier after being evicted when the Watch seized the professor's house. Dressed in ragged clothing.

## Role / Occupation
Former maid. Currently destitute and selling information for coin.

## Personality
Pragmatic — immediately demanded payment before saying anything. Drove a hard bargain, starting negotiations at bronze coins and escalating through multiple shillings. Ultimately sold the lockbox after [[Boris Dorchen]]'s intimidation tactics.

## Relationships
- [[Professor Udo Stradovski]] — former employer

## History with the Party
Met by [[Boris Dorchen]] and [[Joerg Wusterburg]] in [[Session 04 - The Investigation]] at the [[Pit Fight Tavern]]. After payment negotiations and intimidation, sold them a lockbox she had taken from the professor's household for 2 crowns.

## Known Information
- Stradovski was secretive and pretended to be religious — attended services but kept no religious symbols in his home
- Was a homebody; saw patients in a surgery at his house after classes
- The lockbox she sold contained: loose lecture notes, patient notes in medical jargon, and a [[Seuchenbuch (Plague Book)]]
