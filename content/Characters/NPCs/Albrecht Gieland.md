# Albrecht Gieland

## Description
Innkeeper of the Royal Wolf inn in [[Schuten]], Ostland. Runs a strikingly well-organised establishment — soldiers were drilling in the courtyard and rotating watches were posted on the walls when the party arrived. The inn reflects Ostland military culture without being ostentatious about it.

## Role / Occupation
Innkeeper of the Royal Wolf, [[Schuten]].

## Personality
Professional and forthcoming. Confirmed without much prompting that two women and a brutish man had passed through the Royal Wolf three nights prior — this is likely [[Elfrieda Teuschel]]'s group. His well-ordered inn and easy willingness to share information suggests a man who keeps track of who passes through as a matter of course.
