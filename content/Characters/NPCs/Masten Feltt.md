# Masten Feltt

## Description
A merchant whose name was being used by the dead man in the storeroom of the [[Pewter Merchant]]. The body the party examined in [[Session 17 - Asper-ated|Session 17]] was **not** Masten Feltt — it was a commoner in ill-fitting noble's clothes who had presented himself under Feltt's name.

## Role / Occupation
Merchant. The party has not met the real Masten Feltt. Per the innkeeper [[Anton Petz]], the man using the name arrived on horseback with no luggage whatsoever — a detail that in hindsight should have raised immediate suspicion.

## Known Information
- Name given by the dead man at the [[Pewter Merchant]]
- The dead man was a commoner — calloused hands, lice, ill-fitting fine clothes and oversized boots
- Arrived on horseback with no luggage — a detail that should have raised suspicion immediately
- The real Feltt's current whereabouts and true fate are unknown to the party
