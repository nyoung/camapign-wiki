# Ruprecht Sachs

## Description
Apologetic but dutiful man. Was down in the ditch with a ten-foot measuring pole investigating the sewer blockage when attacked.

## Role / Occupation
Surveyor for the [[Department of Public Works]]. Was sent alone to investigate sewage backing up into cellars — apparently the only person the department dispatched after a month of complaints.

## Personality
Dutiful, apologetic. Fought back against the creature despite being badly injured — managed to free his arms and resist the tentacle crushing his midsection.

## Relationships
- [[Department of Public Works]] — employer
- Party — owes them his life; praised them to Watch Sergeant Hintzer

## History with the Party
Rescued from a [[Sewer Horror]] by the party in [[Session 01 - The Sewer Horror]]. Vocally praised them as heroes to [[Udo Hintzer|Watch Sergeant Udo Hintzer]], which led to their reward.

## Known Information
- Sewage had been backing up into cellars in this district for a month before his visit
