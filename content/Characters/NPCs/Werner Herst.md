# Werner Herst

## Description
The dead man in the storeroom of the [[Pewter Merchant]]. The party has examined the body: mid-thirties to early forties, wearing a fine tunic and an expensive pair of purple leather boots, but with calloused hands, dirty fingernails, lice in his hair, and clothes that did not fit him. A commoner in a dead man's clothes.

The party has deduced he was an impostor but has not yet identified him.

## Role / Occupation
Servant — specifically, the servant of the real merchant [[Masten Feltt]].

## Known Information *(to the party)*
- Presented himself at the [[Pewter Merchant]] as the merchant [[Masten Feltt]]
- Arrived on horseback with no luggage
- Was stabbed in a fight with "reivers from the north"; a **young redhead girl** was present just before the violence
- The deceased is a commoner, not a noble

## Secrets / GM Notes
- Per *Pt 1 — A Private War*: Herst is a servant who murdered his master [[Masten Feltt]] on the forest road between the [[Hemp Merchant]] and the [[Pewter Merchant]]. He took Feltt's clothing and horse and rode on to the [[Pewter Merchant]] intending to sell off the goods elsewhere — but the reivers at the inn recognised him (or him as "not Feltt") and a fight broke out in which he was killed
- The real [[Masten Feltt]]'s body is out on the forest road somewhere; no one has found it yet
- Identity can be established by inquiring at the [[Hemp Merchant]] or by [[Felix Antonescu]] (the coroner's assistant) investigating the coaching inns
