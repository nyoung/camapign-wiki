# Gustavus

## Description
A violent raider leader who operates across the Nordland–Ostland border. The party has not met him. [[Christina Asper]] witnessed him **stab the "merchant" in the chest during a scuffle** at the [[Pewter Merchant]] after the man denied knowing the raiders. [[Herga]] — whom his band had kidnapped — described him as violent.

## Role / Occupation
Leader of a band of raiders suspected of operating out of the **[[Heidling Farm]]** roughly fifteen miles north of the [[Pewter Merchant]]. The band crosses the Nordland border to steal livestock and people — Herga was taken while gathering spring berries.

## Relationships
- His raider band — operating out of the [[Heidling Farm]]
- [[Herga]] — kidnapped; intended to be sold to the "merchant"
- The impostor "merchant" at the [[Pewter Merchant]] — killed by Gustavus during the argument

## Known Information
- Raider leader; violent
- Killed the commoner impostor (the Hemp Merchant's servant boy in a merchant's clothes) at the [[Pewter Merchant]] when the man denied the raiders' prior arrangement to buy [[Herga]]
- Witnessed by [[Christina Asper]], who did not intervene
- Suspected to operate out of the [[Heidling Farm]]
