# Udo Hintzer

## Description
Watch Sergeant. Arrived after the sewer fight with a group of watchmen.

## Role / Occupation
Sergeant of the [[The Watch|Middenheim City Watch]].

## Personality
Pragmatic and fair-minded. Rewarded genuine bravery, vouched for the party despite their disheveled, foul-smelling state. Professional — was blowing his whistle frantically during the plaza riot trying to restore order.

## Relationships
- [[The Watch]] — superior/employer
- Party — benefactor; impressed by their actions, gifted them the [[Bone Baton Voucher]]

## History with the Party
Rewarded the party for defeating the [[Sewer Horror]] and rescuing [[Ruprecht Sachs]]. Personally vouched for them at the [[Worm Lamplight]]. Was present (audible) but overwhelmed during the plaza riot at the end of [[Session 01 - The Sewer Horror]].

## Known Information
- Has authority to issue vouchers from the Commission for Trade, Commerce, and Taxation
- Knows the party by sight and has spoken warmly of them
