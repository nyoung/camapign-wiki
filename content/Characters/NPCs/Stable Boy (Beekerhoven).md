# Stable Boy (Beekerhoven)

## Description
An unnamed stable boy working at [[Castor Stark]]'s city stables in [[Beekerhoven]]. Frightened and evasive; needed assurance of protection before he would talk.

## Role / Occupation
Stable hand employed by [[Castor Stark]].

## Personality
Nervous and guarded. Was clearly frightened of [[Marx Khlesl]] and needed both pressure and the promise of [[Temple of Verena|Verenan]] protection before he would reveal what he knew.

## History with the Party

**Session 09 (Beekerhoven):** Initially revealed only that [[Professor Udo Stradovski]] had purchased an old nag, claimed he was heading to Salzenmund, and had a medical bag with him. Observed a suspicious young man in dark clothing who gave the stable boy a nod before vanishing — later identified as connected to Klessel.

**Session 09–10 (under pressure):** After Boris applied pressure and promised Verenan protection, revealed the following: [[Marx Khlesl]] had returned to [[Beekerhoven]] riding the professor's nag; the stable boy had moved the horse elsewhere to prevent [[Castor Stark]] from recognizing it; Klessel was staying at the old military stables being used by the [[Medical Union]].
