# Christina Asper

## Description
A strikingly young witch hunter — perhaps in her mid-twenties — clad in the dark vestments of her order. A wide-brimmed hat sits on her head so perfectly starched it forms a flawless circle. A silver fist entwined with serpents — the symbol of [[Solkan]] — gleams at her chest. She speaks precisely, records everything in a small leather journal in handwriting so uniform it looks printed by a press, and drinks spiced wine. Her eyes are appraising and she does not look away first in any contest of wills.

The party surmised that to achieve full witch hunter rank at her age, she must have done so through exceptional ability and merit alone.

## Role / Occupation
Witch hunter of the [[Fraternal Order of Witch-Hunters]], dispatched by the **University of Altdorf** to assist the party in the pursuit of [[Professor Udo Stradovski]]. Her explicit orders are to **assist, not initiate investigations**. A devout Solkanite — she describes her role not as a judge but as an **executioner**, and her worldview is starkly black and white: discipline, purity, and perfection in all aspects of life.

## Personality
Cold, disciplined, relentless. She made no effort to hide her low opinion of the party as a whole. She listened to [[Pilpoul Ivri|Pilpoul]]'s reckless accusation against her with a steely glare and then, pointedly, gestured for them to sit down and talk. She recorded every detail the party shared with meticulous precision. She has a low tolerance for sidetrack and was pounding on doors at first light the next morning, declaring daylight was burning.

[[Boris Dorchen|Boris]] discovered that the one way to crack her composure was simply to agree with everything she already believed — in her, discipline aligns with obedience to shared principle, and dissent is conflict.

## Relationships
- **University of Altdorf** — her dispatching authority
- **[[Fraternal Order of Witch-Hunters]]** — her order
- **[[Solkan]]** — her god
- **[[Lucas Pollack]]** — fellow witch hunter, left behind in [[Ferlangen]]
- **Party** — ostensibly allied, personally disdainful; most tolerant of [[Boris Dorchen|Boris]]

## History with the Party
Met at the [[Royal Wolf Inn]] in [[Session 17 - Asper-ated|Session 17]]. [[Hoffman Jannitzer]] recognised her on sight. She was immediately accused by [[Pilpoul Ivri|Pilpoul]] of complicity in [[Professor Udo Stradovski|Stradovski]]'s escape; the accusation was withdrawn and smoothed over. She then exchanged intelligence with the party:

**She shared:**
- A noblewoman named [[Elfrieda Teuschel]] is believed to be funding [[Professor Udo Stradovski|Stradovski]]'s activities
- Another witch hunter, [[Lucas Pollack]], has been left in [[Ferlangen]] to track the professor's movements through the region
- A group matching [[Elfrieda Teuschel|Elfrieda]]'s description (dark-haired woman, large male servant) stayed at the [[Royal Wolf Inn]] two nights prior
- The University's view: only a professor expecting execution would flee a Verenan trial; therefore Stradovski is aligned with one of the ruinous powers

**She demanded and took:**
- [[Hildegarde]]'s Khainite-marked crossbow
- [[Hildegarde]]'s black mask
- Covered the dark sword taken from [[Melmoth]] and the Khaine tome with her satchel during examination (effective custody)

On the road to the [[Pewter Merchant]], she allowed [[Pilpoul Ivri|Pilpoul]] to draw her out on her Solkanite beliefs.
