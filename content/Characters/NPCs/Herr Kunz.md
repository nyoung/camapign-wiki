# Herr Kunz

## Description
A guild official at the [[Physicians Guild]] in [[Middenheim]]. Maintains records of all licensed physicians.

## Role / Occupation
Records clerk / official for the Physicians Guild.

## Personality
Methodical and professional. Presented the facts of Stradovski's record without embellishment.

## History with the Party
Met in [[Session 05 - The Road North]] after the party was directed to the Physicians Guild by a court clerk. When shown Stradovski's patient notes, confirmed their extraordinarily positive outcomes.

## Known Information
- Stradovski's record at the guild: completely spotless over **twenty-two years of practice**
- No complaints, no malpractice cases of any kind — the rumour [[Augustus Steiner]] heard was unfounded (or suppressed)
- Stradovski found his own patients; never transferred care when he departed
- The patient notes shown to him confirmed outcomes far beyond normal medical expectation
