# Sir Danderich Flisshausten

## Description
A Knight of the [[Ordo Sancti Inquisita]]. First encountered in the plaza riot where he personally ordered [[Klaus Grebel]]'s arrest; later prosecuted the heresy trial; now leads the Sigmarite contingent pursuing [[Professor Udo Stradovski]].

## Role / Occupation
Knight and Inquisitor of the [[Ordo Sancti Inquisita]]. Claims noble blood.

## Personality
Formal, methodical, and proud of his noble lineage. Forceful about his authority — contested caravan leadership with [[Jurgen Klempf]], acquiescing only with visible resentment. After losing the Grebel trial, personally threatened [[Pilpoul Ivri]] with a crushing handshake. Warned [[Erika Sÿfrid von Drakkenheim]] that her group was gaining "infamy" with the Temple of Sigmar; then invited her to dine — either a social gesture or an attempt to manage her.

## Relationships
- [[Ordo Sancti Inquisita]] — his order
- [[Klaus Grebel]] — ordered his arrest; prosecuted him
- [[Pilpoul Ivri]] — personal enemy; issued a physical threat after the trial
- [[Jurgen Klempf]] — rival; forced to defer on the caravan
- [[Herman Wurt]] — sycophantic companion
- [[Heidi Ubermann]] — subordinate; more capable, resentful of being passed over
- [[Erika Sÿfrid von Drakkenheim]] — dined with her in Arenburg; warned her about party infamy

## History with the Party
- [[Session 01 - The Sewer Horror]]: Led the Ordo contingent in the plaza riot; burst from a nearby building and ordered [[Klaus Grebel]]'s arrest. His red-sashed thugs attacked [[Elke Grebel]].
- [[Session 02 - The Trial of Klaus Grebel]]: Took custody of Grebel; jurisdiction contested by [[Udo Hintzer|Watch Sergeant Udo Hintzer]].
- [[Session 03 - The Trial]]: Prosecuted Klaus's heresy trial at [[Fulpfasser Court]]. Personally threatened [[Pilpoul Ivri]] after losing.
- [[Session 05 - The Road North]]: Argued with [[Jurgen Klempf]] over caravan command; deferred under [[Erika Sÿfrid von Drakkenheim]]'s mediation. Warned Erika about party infamy. Invited her to dinner in [[Arenburg]].
