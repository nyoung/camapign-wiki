# Van Moll

## Description
The innkeeper of the [[Forest Inn]], a hilltop establishment on the road north. Unremarkable in appearance — ordinary enough to be convincing as a country innkeeper, which is precisely why [[Melmoth]] kept him in the role. He was one of only two residents still maintaining a fully human appearance to receive and reassure arriving travellers.

## Role / Occupation
Innkeeper and facade-keeper for [[Melmoth]]'s ghoul operation at the [[Forest Inn]]. One of the original starving locals who participated in the community's initial act of cannibalism two winters past, and has remained part of the operation since — not through violence, but through the continued performance of normality.

## Personality
Obsequious to the point of transparency. He greeted the party with a broad smile, dismissed the visibly ill Gunter as suffering from "the purple flu," blocked [[Hildegarde]] from the kitchen with excessive deference, and deferred every uncomfortable question. His cheerfulness had the slightly too-smooth quality of a man performing a role he has practised many times.

After the battle, he crept out of hiding wringing his hands and bowing nervously, and told the party everything. He had been part of the famine community; he had eaten the dead; he had stayed and kept the inn running while travellers were lured in and murdered. He did not raise a hand in violence himself — but he kept the doors open. He was visibly frightened at the mention of [[Seuchenshof]], going pale and insisting that no one who went there ever came back.

## History with the Party

**Session 13 (Forest Inn):** Greeted the party with a broad smile. Dismissed [[Gunter (Forest Inn)|Gunter]]'s visibly sickly appearance as "the purple flu." Mentioned that [[Professor Udo Stradovski]] had stayed "on the twelfth" — arrived alone and clearly shaken after claiming to have lost his traveling companion to a forest beast. Had barred the inn's gate in mid-afternoon, citing years of beastman and mutant attacks on the road.

Blocked [[Hildegarde]] from entering the kitchen and shouted to someone named "Walbrecht" to bolt the kitchen door.

**Sessions 14–15 (Forest Inn battle):** Was hiding during [[Melmoth]]'s ghoul attack and the subsequent battle.

**Session 15 (aftermath):** Crept out after the battle and revealed the inn's dark history: two winters past, a combination of famine and being snowbound had left the locals desperate. [[Melmoth]] arrived and persuaded the starving residents to consume their dead. This triggered a gradual transformation into ghouls. Since then, travelers have been lured in and murdered. Van Moll and [[Torben Pieknich|Torben]] were kept "normal" to maintain appearances for new arrivals.
