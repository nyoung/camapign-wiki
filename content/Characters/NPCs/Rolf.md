# Rolf

## Description
A small dog, belonging to [[Falconius Thorncobble|Falconius "Connie" Thorncobble]]. His size is comprehensively misleading. He has torn off three toes, bitten deep into a leg, and decapitated a ghoul with a single bite — in different encounters, against different enemies. He has also eaten [[Falconius Thorncobble|Connie]]'s lurker fish with enormous enthusiasm and failed entirely to intimidate a stable boy on cue.

## Role / Occupation
Rat catcher's dog and combat companion to [[Falconius Thorncobble]]. The combat half of this description has proven more significant than expected.

## Personality
Rolf operates on instinct, and his instincts are reliable in a way the party has learned to take seriously. He refused to settle at the [[Forest Inn]] from the moment they arrived — growling at the kitchen bone when someone tried to lure him with it, pressing himself against [[Falconius Thorncobble|Connie]], refusing the scraps he would normally bury his nose in. [[Orazio]] the warhorse shared his unease. Where the humans were uncertain, Rolf had already made his assessment.

In combat, the ferocity is simply a different expression of the same instinct. When he commits, the results are disproportionate to his dimensions. When asked to perform on command — used as a prop for intimidation, for instance — he fails with complete indifference to the social situation around him. He cannot be staged. He can only respond to what is actually there.

## History with the Party

**Session 09 (Beekerhoven):** Failed to intimidate when Connie attempted to use him for effect — a comical disappointment. Shortly after, enthusiastically ate the local lurker fish with great relish.

**Sessions 13–14 (Forest Inn):** Refused to settle at the [[Forest Inn]]. Growled repeatedly at the kitchen bone. Recognized that something was deeply wrong well before any of the humans did.

**Session 11 (Medical Union fight):** Tore off three of [[Hugo (Medical Union)|Hugo]]'s toes during the battle at the [[Medical Union]] compound. Also bit deep into [[Marx Khlesl]]'s leg during the earlier confrontation.

**Session 15 (Forest Inn battle):** Decapitated a ghoul with a single bite.
