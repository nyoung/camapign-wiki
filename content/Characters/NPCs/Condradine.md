# Condradine

## Description
Arrived at the [[Forest Inn]] dressed in travel clothes, claiming to be making the journey from [[Ferlangen]] to [[Middenheim]]. Sharp-eyed from the moment the party entered. Whatever she was — agent, operative, thrall — she barely flinched when [[Hildegarde]]'s crossbow bolt struck her in the leg. She leapt for the rope and missed. A cap was recovered from her body.

## Role / Occupation
Operative for [[Melmoth]]'s ghoul network at the [[Forest Inn]], tasked with isolating vulnerable travellers — specifically those who might be dangerous or alert. [[Hoffman Jannitzer]] was her mark.

## Personality
Condradine was excellent at her work. [[Pilpoul]] watched her specifically, looking for the signs of a pickpocket or a con — and could not find them. She laughed at everything [[Hoffman Jannitzer|Hoffman]] said, sat with her arm around him, and made the attraction look entirely real. This was not careless seduction — it was a performance precise enough to deceive someone who was already suspicious of her.

When [[Boris]] broke through the door, she was calmly overseeing the lowering of an unconscious Hoffman out the window on a rope. No panic, no hesitation, no theatrics. She assessed the situation, took the crossbow bolt without slowing down, jumped for the rope, and missed.

She died through a mistake, not through heroism or capture. Whether she knew what she was working for — whether she was a willing servant of [[Melmoth]]'s cult or something more ambiguous — is unknown. Her inhuman endurance suggests she may not have been fully human by the time the party met her.

## History with the Party

**Session 13 (Forest Inn):** Observed the party's arrival with sharp attention. Told [[Hildegarde]] she had encountered border trouble between Nordland and Ostland but no mutants. Sat with [[Hoffman Jannitzer]] with her arm around him, laughing at everything he said. [[Pilpoul]] watched carefully and determined she was not pickpocketing Hoffman — she seemed to genuinely enjoy his company. Went upstairs with Hoffman at the end of the evening.

**Session 14 (Forest Inn, upper floor):** Was overseeing the lowering of an unconscious [[Hoffman Jannitzer]] out of a window by rope when Boris broke through the door. Shot in the leg by [[Hildegarde]]'s crossbow — barely flinched. Leapt from the window toward the rope — missed. Fell the full height of the building, struck the cart edge with her head, and died.

**Session 16 (aftermath):** A "cap" was recovered from her body after the battle.

## Notes
Her inhuman endurance (barely registering a crossbow bolt to the leg) suggests she may not have been fully human — possibly a thrall, undead, or otherwise supernaturally conditioned.
