# Knight Jürgen Klampf

## Description
A Knight of the [[White Wolves]] — the Ulrican knightly order. Traveling on the caravan north to [[Beekerhoven]] in pursuit of [[Professor Udo Stradovski]].

## Role / Occupation
Knight of the White Wolves. Leads the Ulrican contingent on the caravan.

## Personality
Authoritative and territorial about Ulrican jurisdiction. Argued forcefully with [[Danderich Flisshausten]] that the White Wolves hold authority in Middenheim and Nordland. When [[Erika Sÿfrid von Drakkenheim]] diplomatically suggested he lead with Danderich's advisement given the dangerous forests ahead, he agreed — though the arrangement was tense.

## Relationships
- [[White Wolves]] — his order
- [[Danderich Flisshausten]] — rival; contested leadership of the caravan procession
- [[Erika Sÿfrid von Drakkenheim]] — she brokered the compromise

## History with the Party
Met on the caravan in [[Session 05 - The Road North]]. [[Erika Sÿfrid von Drakkenheim]] de-escalated his standoff with Danderich using noble etiquette.

## Known Information
- Claims White Wolf authority in Middenheim and Nordland
- His men-at-arms travel with the caravan joking and telling war stories (contrasting with the focused Sigmarites)
