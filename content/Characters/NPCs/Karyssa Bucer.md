# Karyssa Bucer

## Description
A woman whose abusive, drunk husband petitioned the community to sell her so she could remarry. Led into the wife sale at the All Shadows Fled inn wearing a halter-like collar on a leash held by her pinched-faced husband. The assembled villagers treated the event as a practical community custom rather than a scandal — it was [[Hildegarde]]'s bid on her behalf, and her subsequent fury at losing, that stood apart from the room's mood.

## Personality / Situation
Karyssa's situation was accepted as ordinary by the community around her. She was married to a drunk who mistreated her; the wife sale was a recognised resolution. [[Hildegarde]] attempted to outbid [[Wilhelm Teggetthof]] on her behalf at 4 silver shillings and 16 brass pennies, but the innkeeper prevailed at 5 shillings and married her the same evening by a local Ulrican priest. Whether this outcome was better than what [[Hildegarde]] intended is unclear — but it was presumably better than what she was leaving.

## History with the Party

**Session 12 (All Shadows Fled inn):** Put up for sale in a traditional wife sale. [[Hildegarde]] attempted to bid for her — offering 4 silver shillings and 16 brass pennies — in what appeared to be an attempt to secure her freedom outright. [[Wilhelm Teggetthof]], the innkeeper, outbid Hildegarde at 5 silver shillings and purchased her himself.

Hildegarde stormed out in fury after losing the bid.

Karyssa was married to [[Wilhelm Teggetthof]] that same evening by a local Ulrican priest. The assembled villagers treated the affair as an ordinary practical custom.

## Notes
Now wife of [[Wilhelm Teggetthof]] and presumably managing the All Shadows Fled inn with him.
