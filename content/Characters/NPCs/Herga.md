# Herga

## Description
A young woman from a Nordland family farm, kidnapped by raiders while gathering spring berries. Bruises and scratches along her arms; otherwise unrestrained when the party found her at the [[Grunwald Farm]]. Walks freely wrapped in her cloak after being freed.

## Role / Occupation
A farm girl. She was taken because the raiders had agreed to sell her to a supposed merchant at the [[Pewter Merchant]] — an arrangement that collapsed when the "merchant" denied all knowledge of it and was killed by the raider leader **[[Gustavus]]**.

## Personality
Observant. Provided the clearest account of the Pewter Merchant killing to date — noting the "merchant" did not speak like a noble or an educated man, but like someone from a local farm, an observation that deepened the party's suspicion the dead man was an impostor.

## Relationships
- Family on a Nordland farm
- [[Hildegarde]] — gave her a spare dagger for self-defence
- [[Boris Dorchen|Boris]] — quietly provided two gold crowns' worth of jewellery as a symbolic payment to the Grunwald family
- The [[Grunwald Farm]] family — held her, intending to sell her as a servant in autumn for around six crowns
- The raiders at the [[Heidling Farm]] — her abductors

## History with the Party
**Session 18:** Party arrived at the [[Grunwald Farm]] on foot from the [[Pewter Merchant]]. After [[Hildegarde]]'s charm and bounty-hunter's badge got them through the gate, the farmer's wife admitted they were holding a young woman. The farmer tried to raise his asking price from six to eight crowns; [[Hildegarde]] threatened to report the family for slavery and invoked the nearby witch hunter, and [[Berin Garibaldi|Berin]] loosened his sword in its scabbard. Herga was released without payment. She gave a formal written statement at the inn, received a spare dagger, and agreed to travel with the party to [[Ferlangen]], where she will find refuge at the [[Temple of Shallya]] and eventually make her way safely home.

## Known Information
- Nordland farm girl; kidnapped while gathering spring berries
- Raiders led by **[[Gustavus]]** planned to sell her to a "merchant" at the [[Pewter Merchant]]
- The "merchant" denied knowing the raiders, prompting the argument that led to his killing
- Raiders were likely from the **[[Heidling Farm]]** about fifteen miles north — known for crossing the border to steal livestock and people
- Her affidavit is now in the hands of [[Anton Petz]]
