# Professor Humpis

## Description
A professor of law at the [[Collegium Theologica]]. The original accuser of [[Professor Udo Stradovski]]. His office features warm candlelight and perfectly aligned theological texts.

## Role / Occupation
Professor of Law. Filed the accusation of heresy against Stradovski.

## Personality
Sharp and combative. Immediately saw through [[Pilpoul Ivri]]'s attempt to frame his questioning as "trial preparation" — dismissed it as nonsense and refused to elaborate on case details. Grew increasingly annoyed under pressure. Expressed genuine bewilderment at why Stradovski fled, noting the accusations were not severe enough to warrant it.

## Relationships
- [[Collegium Theologica]] — faculty
- [[Professor Udo Stradovski]] — accused; colleagues for ~20 years despite apparent lack of familiarity

## History with the Party
Interviewed by [[Pilpoul Ivri]], [[Hildegarde]], and [[Erika Sÿfrid von Drakkenheim]] in [[Session 04 - The Investigation]]. Was uncooperative and eventually had them removed from his office.

## Known Information
- Filed the original heresy accusation against Stradovski
- Heresy charge: Stradovski claimed gods do not exist and priests are merely magicians
- Genuinely puzzled by Stradovski's flight — considers the accusations not severe enough to warrant abandoning his position
- Stradovski was extremely private; not well-known by faculty despite 20 years at the Collegium

## Secrets / GM Notes
- Why did he file the accusation? Personal rivalry? Genuine theological offence? External pressure?
- His bewilderment at the flight could be genuine or performed
