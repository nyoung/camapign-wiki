# Elson Teuschel

## Description
Middle-aged minter of [[Beekerhoven]], notably older than his wife [[Elfrida Teuschel]]. Lives in the large house with pillars on Schlosse de Vies Street — the same house [[Elfrida Teuschel|Elfrida]] has now abandoned. When the party returned to report on the investigation's progress, he answered the door dishevelled and visibly distressed.

## Role / Occupation
Minter — responsible for the town's coin production. Now dealing with the aftermath of a large mint gold theft and his wife's disappearance.

## Personality
Open and talkative where [[Elfrida Teuschel|Elfrida]] was guarded — the contrast between them was noticeable from the first meeting. He accepted 6 shillings as alms from [[Berin Garibaldi]] over Elfrida's obvious displeasure, and it was the generosity she complained about as the party left. He appears to have known nothing of what his wife was involved in; he believed the earlier coin thefts were the work of a mint apprentice. When Elfrida vanished with a large shipment of mint gold, he reported it dutifully and appeared genuinely devastated. He is honest and probably oblivious, and has now been left alone with the consequences of a conspiracy he never saw coming.

## Relationships
- [[Elfrida Teuschel]] — wife
- [[William Coin]] — household servant
- [[Professor Udo Stradovski]] — treated Elfrida years ago; was a recent houseguest

## History with the Party
- [[Session 08 - The Minter's Wife]]: Met [[Berin Garibaldi]] and [[Hildegarde]] at the door. Confirmed Stradovski's treatment of Elfrida cost "enough gold to buy a nice horse." Revealed that the professor appeared unexpectedly on their doorstep approximately a week and a half ago (c. 11 Nachexen 2512), stayed one night, and departed. Also mentioned that a mint apprentice had recently absconded with freshly minted coins, contributing to the town's financial troubles. Accepted 6 shillings as alms from Berin.

## Known Information
- Stradovski's treatment of Elfrida cost a substantial sum (multiple gold crowns)
- Stradovski visited ~11 Nachexen 2512, stayed one night, departed the next morning
- A mint apprentice absconded with freshly minted coins — the town is under financial strain
