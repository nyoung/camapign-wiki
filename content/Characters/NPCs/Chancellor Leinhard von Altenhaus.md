# Chancellor Leinhard von Altenhaus

## Description
The Chancellor of the [[Collegium Theologica]]. Received the party in a polished office smelling of old parchment and sealing wax.

## Role / Occupation
Chancellor — head administrator of the Collegium Theologica.

## Personality
Formal and dismissive. Characterised the whole Stradovski affair as "embarrassing and nonsensical." Willing to provide basic information and arrange student guides, but eager to distance the institution from the scandal.

## Relationships
- [[Collegium Theologica]] — head
- [[Professor Udo Stradovski]] — former faculty member
- [[Professor Humpis]] — faculty under his authority

## History with the Party
Met in [[Session 04 - The Investigation]]. Confirmed Stradovski's role at the Collegium and arranged for the student [[Stellman (Student Guide)]] to escort the party.

## Known Information
- Stradovski was a medical professor and follower of [[Cult of Shallya|Shallya]]
- Stradovski specialised in disease study and was known for miraculous cures of terminally ill patients
- Claims little knowledge of Stradovski's personal life or methods
- Considers the situation embarrassing for the institution

## Secrets / GM Notes
- Is his dismissiveness genuine indifference, or is he protecting the Collegium's reputation?
