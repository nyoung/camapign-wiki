# Augustus Steiner

## Description
Son of [[Lady Steiner]]. A young noble or student. Currently imprisoned in the [[Collegium Theologica]]'s cells. Approached the bars hopefully when the party arrived, asking if the professor had returned.

## Role / Occupation
Student. Was mentored by [[Professor Udo Stradovski]].

## Personality
Hopeful but confused. Has hazy memories of agreeing to act as bond — the party suspects he was drugged by Stradovski to secure his compliance.

## Relationships
- [[Lady Steiner]] — mother
- [[Professor Udo Stradovski]] — mentor; likely drugged him to facilitate escape

## History with the Party
- Mentioned in [[Session 02 - The Trial of Klaus Grebel]]: his imprisonment is the basis of the quest.
- [[Session 04 - The Investigation]]: Met in person through the cell bars. Questioned by [[Erika Sÿfrid von Drakkenheim]].

## Known Information
- Has no clear memory of agreeing to be Stradovski's bond — recalls being called to the cells, having a conversation through the bars, but details are confused and hazy
- Tea was served during that conversation — the party suspects it was drugged
- Describes Stradovski as secretive and focused on work; rarely discussed personal matters or cases
- Heard rumours of a patient suing Stradovski for medical negligence
- Confirms Stradovski had a **patient from [[Beekerhoven]]** — a concrete lead
- Faces execution in ~6 weeks from 18 Nachexen 2512 if the professor is not found
