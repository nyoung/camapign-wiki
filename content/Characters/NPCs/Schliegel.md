# Schliegel

## Description
A farmhand. Never encountered directly — identified through interrogation.

## History with the Party
Not encountered directly.

**Session 11 (interrogation of [[Marx Khlesl]]):** Klessel named Schliegel as the person who actually accompanied [[Professor Udo Stradovski]] when the professor left [[Beekerhoven]] heading north — contradicting the false trail Klessel had laid toward Salzenmund.

## Notes
Stradovski arrived at the [[Forest Inn]] alone, claiming to have "lost his traveling companion to a forest beast." Session 15 revealed that Stradovski had negotiated his own release from [[Melmoth]] by sacrificing a companion along with a horse and sharing knowledge of ghoul care. Schliegel is presumed to be this sacrificed companion and is likely dead.
