# Wilhelm Teggethof

## Description
Innkeeper of the All Shadows Fled inn on the road north.

## Role / Occupation
Innkeeper. Officiated and participated in the traditional wife sale of [[Karyssa Bucer]].

## Personality
Friendly and pragmatic. Approached the wife sale as a practical community custom — and saw in it an opportunity to acquire a capable inn manager.

## History with the Party

**Session 12 (All Shadows Fled inn):** Announced and conducted the traditional wife sale of [[Karyssa Bucer|Karyssa]], whose abusive, drunk husband had petitioned the community to sell her so she could remarry. Outbid [[Hildegarde]] (who offered 4 silver shillings and 16 brass pennies) by purchasing Karyssa himself for 5 silver shillings.

Claimed Karyssa would be better treated managing the All Shadows Fled with him. Married her that same evening via a local Ulrican priest.
