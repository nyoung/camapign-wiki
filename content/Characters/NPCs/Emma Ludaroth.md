# Emma Ludaroth

## Description
Innkeeper of the [[Fox and Crown]]. Appeared dishevelled and sweating when the party presented the results of the were-rabbit hunt — presumably a stressful night.

## Role / Occupation
Innkeeper of the Fox and Crown.

## History with the Party
- [[Session 07 - The Were-Rabbit's End]]: Hired the party (via the locals) to deal with the [[Were-Rabbit]] raiding the inn's vegetable stores. Confirmed the produce was intact after the battle and paid Boris 5 Gold Crowns as promised. The morning after the battle, [[Hildegarde]] asked Emma to spread the tale of the were-rabbit hunt to build [[Boris Dorchen]]'s reputation.

## Known Information
- Offered: complimentary ale, rabbit stew, and rooms with rabbit fur blankets as part of the reward
- Agreed to spread word of Boris's reputation as a rabbit hunter
