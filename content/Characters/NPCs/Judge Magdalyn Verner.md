# Judge Magdalyn Verner

## Description
Middle-aged woman of stern demeanor. Presides at [[Fulpfasser Court]].

## Role / Occupation
Judge. Presided over [[Klaus Grebel]]'s heresy trial.

## Personality
Stern but fair. Reprimanded [[Pilpoul Ivri|Pilpoul]] for grandstanding during cross-examination and fined him 5 shillings, sixpence. Granted a recess when Pilpoul raised the valid point about pre-trial disclosure. Ultimately weighed the evidence carefully and chose leniency given the circumstances.

## History with the Party
Presided over Klaus's trial in [[Session 03 - The Trial]]. Fined [[Pilpoul Ivri|Pilpoul]] 5 shillings, sixpence for grandstanding. Delivered a verdict of guilty but sentenced Klaus to community service rather than the full heretical judgment. After the trial, [[Pilpoul Ivri|Pilpoul]] thanked her for her wisdom.
