# Middenland Militia

## Overview
The provincial militia of Middenland. The party became provisional members by accepting the [[Find Professor Stradovski]] warrant. Administered through the Clerk's Office (a building bearing the scales of Verena and the wolf of Ulric).

## Goals
Defence of Middenheim and Middenland. Uphold the city's laws and obey lawful commands.

## Structure / Leadership
Unknown — administered through clerks for warrant purposes.

## Notable Members
- All party members — provisional members
- [[Joerg Wusterburg]] — provisional member

## Resources & Influence
- Basic pay: 8 shillings/day (standard), 10 shillings/day (specialist)
- Advance pay: 6 Gold Crowns per member for the first fortnight
- Optional militia equipment available: crossbow, mail shirt, shield, sword (5 GC deposit)
- Provisions wagon available if party needs to leave the city
- Watch sergeant escort available for accessing [[Professor Udo Stradovski]]'s house

## Relationship with the Party
Employer. Party swore fealty to Middenheim and accepted provisional militia status.

## Known Activities
- Issued the warrant for [[Find Professor Stradovski]]
- Provided advance pay and optional equipment to the party

## Notes
- Party must not lose their militia passes — no pass means no further payment
- Oath sworn: loyalty, honour, defence of city and people, uphold laws, obey lawful commands, service until honourable discharge or death
