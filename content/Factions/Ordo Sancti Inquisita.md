# Ordo Sancti Inquisita

## Overview
A Sigmarite knightly order with inquisitorial powers. Operating in [[Middenheim]] despite it being an Ulrican city. Instigated the plaza riot in Sessions 01–02, prosecuted [[Klaus Grebel]]'s heresy trial, and are actively hunting [[Professor Udo Stradovski]].

## Goals
Enforce Sigmarite orthodoxy. Hunt and execute those accused of heresy or blasphemy. Extend Sigmarite authority into Ulrican territories.

## Structure / Leadership
- [[Danderich Flisshausten]] — Knight; instigated the plaza riot (Sessions 01–02), prosecuted the Grebel trial, leads the caravan contingent north

## Notable Members
- [[Danderich Flisshausten]]
- [[Heinricus Ralf]] — allied Sigmarite priest (expert witness at trial)
- [[Herman Wurt]] — sycophantic agent; eyewitness at trial
- [[Heidi Ubermann]] — capable but passed over; possible weak link
- Red-sash thugs (unnamed)

## Resources & Influence
Knights, street muscle, legal standing to bring prosecutions. Willing to fabricate or collude on affidavits. Enough authority to operate in an Ulrican city, though jurisdiction is contested by [[The Watch]].

## Relationship with the Party
Hostile. [[Danderich Flisshausten]] personally threatened [[Pilpoul Ivri]] after losing the Grebel trial and warned [[Erika Sÿfrid von Drakkenheim]] that the party is gaining "infamy" with the Temple of Sigmar.

## Known Activities
- Instigated the plaza riot (Sessions 01–02)
- Arrested [[Klaus Grebel]]
- Prosecuted Klaus's heresy trial — presented likely-fabricated affidavits
- Hunting [[Professor Udo Stradovski]] (to execute)

## Notes
- Affidavits presented at trial appeared to be written by one hand with different signatures — evidence of internal fabrication
- Their legal authority in Middenheim is not unchallenged; [[Udo Hintzer|Watch Sergeant Udo Hintzer]] successfully contested jurisdiction over Klaus's arrest
