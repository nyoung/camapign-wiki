# Cult of Ulric

## Overview
The dominant religion of [[Middenheim]]. Ulric is the god of wolves, winter, and battle. His cult holds significant political and cultural power in the city.

## Goals
Maintain Ulric's primacy in Middenheim and the North. Resist Sigmarite encroachment.
