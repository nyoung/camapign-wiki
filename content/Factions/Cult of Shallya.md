# Cult of Shallya

## Overview
The faith of Shallya, goddess of mercy, healing, and compassion. Shallyan priests and novices wear white habits and are devoted to healing and alleviating suffering.

## Goals
Heal the sick and suffering. Practice and promote mercy and compassion.
