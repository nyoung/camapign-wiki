# Physicians Guild

## Overview
The guild overseeing licensed physicians in [[Middenheim]]. Medical malpractice falls under its jurisdiction rather than the courts. Maintains records of all licensed physicians.

## Structure / Leadership
- [[Herr Kunz]] — guild official (records)

## Resources & Influence
Legal jurisdiction over medical practice and malpractice. Comprehensive records of licensed physicians.

## Known Activities
- Confirmed [[Professor Udo Stradovski]]'s spotless record over 22 years

## Notes
- The malpractice rumour [[Augustus Steiner]] mentioned has no basis in guild records — either the rumour was false or the matter was handled outside official channels
