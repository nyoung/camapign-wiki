# Medical Union

## Overview
A highly successful cell of Nurgle cultists operating within the Imperial medical community. Members would reject the label of cultists — their doctrine holds that studying and harnessing disease can produce cures and beneficial effects. The cult has infiltrated senior positions within the Physicians Guild and has connections reaching into the Cult of Shallya.

## Philosophy
Rather than the typical image of infected rabble, the Medical Union consists of medical professionals who believe disease can be weaponised against infection itself. They have developed treatments that produce apparently miraculous cures — including the ability to make patients appear younger and healthier. These treatments are part natural, part mystical, and taint recipients with the essence of Nurgle whether they realise it or not.
