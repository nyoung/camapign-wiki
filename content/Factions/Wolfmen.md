# Wolfmen

## Overview
A street gang operating in [[Beekerhoven]], involved in extortion and intimidation. They maintain a line against murder and arson. Led by [[Marx Khlesl]], who operates above the street-level work and uses the gang to project power.

## Known Members
- [[Marx Khlesl]] — boss

## History with the Party
The party encountered the Wolfmen's influence during their investigation in [[Beekerhoven]]. The [[Temple of Verena (Beekerhoven)]] priestess identified Khlesl as the gang's leader.
