# Sewer Horror

## Description
A massive octopus-like creature, chaos-tainted. So large it was completely jammed within the narrow sewer shaft beneath [[Middenheim]]'s city wall, unable to emerge fully. Its only means of attack were the black-gray tentacles it could force through the iron bars of the sewer grating. Emitted a sour, throat-stinging stench. Black inky fluid leaked from wounded tentacles. Discharged foul-smelling fluid on death.
