# Session 06 — The Were-Rabbit

**Played:** 3 January 2026
**In-game date:** 21 Nachexen 2512

## Summary

The party's departure from the area south of [[Fox and Crown]] was delayed when the road was found torn up overnight — impassable by wheeled vehicles. The party rallied the locals to repair it before the caravan could move, and they arrived at the Fox and Crown after sundown on 21 Nachexen 2512.

The inn's locals had a problem: a [[Were-Rabbit]] had been raiding their vegetable stores. When they heard that [[Boris Dorchen]] claimed to have hunted a were-rabbit in the Drakwald (originally mentioned in [[Session 01 - The Sewer Horror]], and met with considerable mockery at the time), they immediately hired him to deal with it. The job was accepted.

The party set an ambush at the compound and waited through the night.

## Key Events

### Road Repair
- The road between [[Arenburg]] and the Fox and Crown had been torn up overnight — likely by the were-rabbit's burrowing
- Party organised locals to fill the ruts and restore the road; departed late as a result

### The Ambush Setup
- Party took positions throughout the Fox and Crown compound
- [[Boris Dorchen]] — elevated position at a second-floor window
- [[Joerg Wusterburg]] — rooftop position
- [[Hildegarde]] — crossbow, covering the compound
- [[Pilpoul Ivri]] — cover behind a produce wagon
- [[Erika Sÿfrid von Drakkenheim]] — defensive position near a cart
- [[Hoffman Jannitzer]] — covering the gate

### The Attack
- Rabbit swarms struck first: dozens to hundreds of ordinary rabbits burrowing under the south gate and through holes in the east wall, swarming into the compound
- The [[Were-Rabbit]] itself appeared on the rooftop directly above Joerg's position
- It leaped to the kitchen roof, then launched a diving charge straight at Joerg — claws narrowly missed
- Boris opened fire from his window, effectively thinning the swarms
- Hildegarde fumbled her initial crossbow reload; subsequently landed a shot that scattered one swarm
- Joerg attempted upward stabs through the roof shingles before the creature charged him; came face-to-face with the beast and narrowly evaded
- Pilpoul attempted to rally the party from behind the wagon
- Erika kicked away attacking rabbits with her shield
- Hoffman got off one shot and was running to assist when the session ended

## Session End State

**Combat unresolved — session ended mid-fight.** The were-rabbit is mobile and dangerous; swarms continue to pressure multiple party members simultaneously.

> *"As Hoffman came running to assist, the true battle was only just beginning."*
