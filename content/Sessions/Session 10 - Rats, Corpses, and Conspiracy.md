# Session 10 — Rats, Corpses, and Conspiracy

**Played:** 14 February 2026
**In-game date:** 25 Nachexen 2512

## Summary

The party conducted reconnaissance on the [[Medical Union]] compound and then infiltrated it under cover of a rat-catching job. Inside, [[Falconius Thorncobble|Connie]] and [[Boris Dorchen]] discovered a makeshift alchemy laboratory, two shrouded corpses, a dissected body, and signs of tunnelling through the city wall. Most critically, Connie found a letter revealing that [[Professor Udo Stradovski|the professor]] was heading to [[Ferlangen]] — not [[Salzenmund]] as previously believed. The session ended in a brutal and chaotic melee as [[Marx Khlesl]] discovered the intruders and attempted to seize them.

## Key Events

### Continued Intelligence Gathering at the City Stables

- The party returned to the [[City Stables (Beekerhoven)]] and pressed the stable boy further
- The stable boy confirmed: [[Marx Khlesl]] had returned riding the professor's old nag just two days after three riders — including [[Professor Udo Stradovski|the professor]] — had departed within half an hour of each other, all paying with freshly minted coins
- This implied the professor had acquired a faster horse before leaving town
- The stable boy disclosed that [[Marx Khlesl]] was associated with the [[Medical Union]], operating out of a fortified former military stable against the city wall
- The party reassured the frightened stable boy of Verenan protection and sent him on his way

### Scouting the Medical Union Compound

- The group split up to gather intelligence on the compound
- [[Falconius Thorncobble|Connie]] and [[Rolf]] stealthily scouted the perimeter: well-maintained walls, a guard tower, and bored sentries patrolling; the compound appeared well-fortified
- [[Berin Garibaldi|Berin]], drawing on military knowledge, estimated eight to ten men would be needed to properly defend the fortification
- [[Pilpoul Ivri]] and [[Boris Dorchen]] worked the streets gathering gossip from local merchants and townspeople
  - [[Urs Huber]] had purchased the old military stable from the town council and used it as a base for selling medical supplies; locals viewed the operation with deep suspicion
  - The town's only doctor, [[Dr. Thurne]], had been murdered six months prior in what was officially ruled a bungled burglary; many believed the [[Medical Union]] had framed an innocent peddler named [[Oslin]] for the crime

### Infiltration Under Cover (rat catchers)

- The party decided infiltration was their best option
- [[Falconius Thorncobble|Connie]] and [[Boris Dorchen]] approached the compound gates posing as rat catchers seeking work
- After tense negotiation with [[Urs Huber]] — described as resembling an older version of a well-known actor — they were granted entry under the watchful eye of a mousy young woman named [[Elsie (Medical Union)|Elsie]]
- Inside the compound: old stables, disturbed ground suggesting fresh graves, and an alarming number of rats; [[Rolf]] efficiently caught vermin, reinforcing the cover story

### Discovery in the Laboratory

- [[Elsie (Medical Union)|Elsie]] led Boris and Connie to a makeshift alchemy laboratory within the stable building
- Inside: a body lying motionless on a table, various glass apparatus producing noxious liquids, and two shrouded corpses on the ground being gnawed by rats
- Signs of excavation were visible into the city wall itself — as though someone had been tunnelling through it
- [[Boris Dorchen]] and [[Rolf]] continued the rat-catching cover while [[Falconius Thorncobble|Connie]] slipped away to investigate

### Connie's Discovery — The Ferlangen Letter

- [[Falconius Thorncobble|Connie]] slipped into the living quarters in the main stone building
- Among the debris and makeshift beds, he found a small table covered with papers and correspondence
- By candlelight, he discovered a crucial note addressed to "Marx" instructing him to prepare provisions for "the professor" for a journey to [[Ferlangen]] — not [[Salzenmund]]
- The note was signed by [[Elfrieda Teuschel|E. Teuschel]] — the [[Physicians Guild|Mintner]]'s wife, who had provided the professor with freshly minted coins
- Connie pocketed the letter as evidence and returned to Boris

### The Melee Begins

- [[Marx Khlesl]] burst into the stable, his face contorted with rage at discovering intruders who had seen too much; he lunged for [[Falconius Thorncobble|Connie]], attempting to grab him by the collar
- [[Boris Dorchen]] intervened with improvised brilliance: using his rat-laden pole as a flail, he struck Marks and knocked him off balance — the dangling rats scratching and clawing at Marks's face
- [[Rolf]] joined the fray with vicious enthusiasm, sinking his teeth deep into Marks's leg and refusing to let go despite his small size
- [[Elsie (Medical Union)|Elsie]] attacked Boris from behind before retreating
- A tall, gaunt man named [[Hugo (Medical Union)|Hugo]] charged in wielding a spiked club; his brutal strike nearly felled [[Falconius Thorncobble|Connie]], who barely escaped the building through luck and desperation

### The Exterior Fight

- Outside the compound, [[Berin Garibaldi|Berin]] heard the commotion and immediately mounted [[Orazio]], charging toward the compound gate
- His steed's powerful rear kick failed to break through the reinforced gate door; Berin climbed atop the gate wall to gain a vantage point
- [[Pilpoul Ivri]] approached from another angle, attempting to confuse [[Urs Huber]] with talk of purchasing medical supplies; [[Hoffman Jannitzer]] huffed along behind him
- Inside, [[Boris Dorchen]] demonstrated tactical brilliance: he grabbed the stunned and bleeding [[Marx Khlesl]], pressed his axe to the man's throat, and used him as a human shield while demanding safe passage
- As [[Berin Garibaldi|Berin]] opened the gate from above and the party converged on the compound, another guard struck [[Pilpoul Ivri]] with knuckle dusters, leaving the scholar barely standing
- The session ended mid-confrontation, with Boris holding Marks at axe-point and the situation unresolved

- The party is mid-battle at session end; several members have taken wounds
