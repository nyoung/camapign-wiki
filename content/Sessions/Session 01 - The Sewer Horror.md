# Session 01 — The Sewer Horror

## Summary
The party met as strangers near Middenheim's city wall and were drawn together by a chaos-tainted sewer creature attacking a surveyor. After defeating it, they were rewarded by Watch Sergeant Udo Hintzer and briefly enjoyed hospitality at the Worm Lamplight — until a street agitator's arrest sparked a violent clash between Ulricans and Sigmarites in the plaza outside.

## Events

### The Sewer Attack
A surveyor, [[Ruprecht Sachs]], was investigating a backed-up sewer near the city wall when a massive chaos-tainted octopus-like creature jammed in the sewer shaft began attacking him through the iron grating with its tentacles. The party — strangers to one another — all moved toward the danger while townsfolk fled.

- [[Pilpoul]] rushed down into the ditch, fashioning a makeshift rope from vines and debris to reach Sachs; became entangled himself when the tentacle wrapped around his leg.
- [[Boris]] used his sling with precision, repeatedly striking the creature.
- [[Hildegarde]] sprinted down and threw herself onto a tentacle, attempting to pin and cut it.
- [[Altus]] vaulted the railing and tackled [[Pilpoul]] free from the tentacle, shielding him.

The creature, unable to emerge fully from the shaft, was destroyed when [[Boris]]'s final sling stone struck true. Its tentacles went limp and discharged foul fluid. The party suffered contact with chaos-tainted material.

### The Reward
[[Watch Sergeant Udo Hintzer]] arrived with watchmen after the battle. [[Ruprecht Sachs]] praised the party as heroes. Hintzer presented them with a [[Bone Baton Voucher]] — worth 5 crowns from the Commission for Trade, Commerce, and Taxation — and escorted them to the [[Worm Lamplight]] tavern despite their foul state.

Over ale, the party introduced themselves. [[Altus]] downplayed his role. [[Hildegarde]] deflected personal questions. [[Boris]] spoke of his life in the Drakwald and his hunting of dangerous creatures (including, allegedly, a wererabbit). A brief moment of camaraderie formed.

### Eviction and the Broken Window
The barkeep secured the tavern and asked the party to leave, citing their smell and the sergeant's absence. [[Altus]], insulted, waited for a distraction and hurled a large stone through the tavern window, shouting about Ulric being the true power in Middenheim.

### The Plaza Riot
Outside, [[Klaus Grebel]] — a young agitator — was delivering a speech about merchant and guild oppression from atop stacked crates. A [[Elke Grebel]] tried anxiously to get him to stop. Men in red sashes (Sigmarite-affiliated thugs) circled the crowd. An [[Danderich Flisshausten]] burst from a nearby building, accused Grebel of profaning Sigmar's name, and ordered his arrest.

- A thug grabbed Grebel; another knocked the Shallyan novice to the ground.
- Ruffians in the crowd began throwing rocks at the Sigmarite thugs, shouting "Sigmarite scum."
- [[Boris]], outraged at the mistreatment of the Shallyan sister, charged in fists-first.
- [[Hildegarde]] attempted to climb to a high vantage to calm the crowd.
- [[Altus]] positioned himself away from the worst of it.
- [[Pilpoul]] pointed Grebel out to the Inquisitor.
- [[Watch Sergeant Udo Hintzer]] could be heard blowing his whistle frantically in the distance.

**Session ends mid-riot.**
