# Session 15 — Stradovski's Guide to Selling Out Your Friends

**Played:** 21 March 2026
**In-game date:** ~2–3 Jahrdrung 2512 (approx., early morning continuing from Session 14)

## Summary

The battle to recover [[Hoffman Janitzer]] from the ghoulish kidnappers concluded outside the [[Forest Inn]]. [[Hildegarde]] was critically wounded — shot twice and nearly killed — while [[Berin Garibaldi]] and [[Orazio]] drove through the gate in a charging assault that broke the enemy. The dark priest [[Melmoth]] was slain and beheaded. [[Torben Pieknich]] was captured. [[Hoffman Janitzer]] was recovered alive but unconscious. In the aftermath, the innkeeper [[Van Moll]] finally revealed the ghoulish history of the inn — and under firm interrogation, [[Torben Pieknich]] disclosed a deeply troubling truth: [[Professor Udo Stradovski]] had passed through this very inn roughly twelve days prior, immediately recognised the ghoul operation, and coldly negotiated his own safe passage by handing over his travelling companion and a horse to [[Melmoth]] — and by sharing academic knowledge about ghoul creation.

## Key Events

### The Battle Continues Outside the Gate

- [[Boris Dorchen]] sprinted toward the inn's exit, seeking a clear shot at the retreating kidnappers
- [[Pilpoul Ivri]] charged through the open gate to meet the enemy directly
- A ghoulish figure rushed [[Berin Garibaldi]] and [[Orazio]], tearing savagely at the horse's neck and chest; Berin wheeled and struck back in a furious exchange
- [[Hildegarde]], burning with rage, charged toward the cart carrying the unconscious [[Hoffman Janitzer]] — hidden crossbowmen revealed themselves and loosed bolts, **striking her twice in the lower body** and sending her crashing to the ground
- Even prone and bleeding, Hildegarde clawed forward through sheer force of will, refusing to yield

### Decisive Blows

- [[Rolf]] launched himself into the fray and **decapitated a stunned ghoul in a single savage bite**
- [[Pilpoul Ivri]] used a clever verbal ruse and a well-timed slam against the fence to draw enemy sniper fire, then cracked a ghoulish attacker with a critical sling stone — the creature crumpled
- [[Berin Garibaldi]] spurred [[Orazio]] through the gateway in a thundering charge, the warhorse trampling assailants; Berin brought his sword down in a devastating arc, **pinning the skilled swordsman against the wall with lethal force**

### Melmoth Slain; Torben Captured

- The masked leader of the attackers — **[[Melmoth]]**, clad in crimson and black — was killed in the fighting
- [[Berin Garibaldi]] grimly severed Melmoth's head and **placed it upon the gatepost as a warning**
- [[Torben Pieknich]], revealed to have been posing as a road warden while serving Melmoth, was captured, bound, and dragged inside for questioning
- [[Hoffman Janitzer]] was found alive but deeply unconscious — drugged or poisoned

### Aftermath and Triage

- [[Hildegarde]] was bleeding from a neck wound that had come terrifyingly close to ending her life; she managed a defiant thumbs up from the stable floor before retreating inside to avoid further crossbow fire
- [[Berin Garibaldi]] cut a strip from his own cloak and pressed it against Hildegarde's wound, working quickly to stop the bleeding; he then tended [[Orazio]]'s injuries
- Hildegarde, half-delirious, rasped out a single question — whether Hoffman had been saved
- [[Pilpoul Ivri]], in characteristic fashion, assured her he would have let her die, but that she would not die in vain

### The Loot of Melmoth

Items recovered from [[Melmoth]]'s body:
- A thick bag of gold crowns from Imperial, Nordland, and Ostland mints
- Ornate weapons etched with unsettling glyphs
- Silver knuckle dusters
- A small shield bearing a **red skull**
- The tome **Tenebris Geheimnis** (Secrets of the Dark)

[[Falconius Thorncobble]] thumbed through *Tenebris Geheimnis* with growing unease, discovering it contained:
- The theology of the god **Khaine**
- Recipes for poisons
- Detailed instructions for attaining undeath as a lich, vampire, or mummy

Connie called [[Berin Garibaldi]] over and shared the contents in hushed tones; both exchanged grave looks.

### Van Moll's Confession

The innkeeper [[Van Moll]] crept out from hiding, wringing his hands, and explained the dark history of the [[Forest Inn]]:

- **Two winters prior**: a famine left the locals starving and snowbound
- [[Melmoth]] arrived at that moment and convinced desperate townsfolk to **consume their dead** — a practice that slowly transformed them into ghouls
- Since then, travellers had been lured in and murdered, fed to the growing ghoul army
- [[Van Moll]] and [[Torben Pieknich]] were kept as seemingly normal humans to maintain appearances and run the front

### The Revelation About Stradovski

Under [[Berin Garibaldi]]'s firm and intimidating questioning, [[Torben Pieknich]] finally broke and revealed:

- **[[Professor Udo Stradovski]] passed through the Forest Inn roughly twelve days prior**
- He immediately recognised the ghoulish nature of the inn's staff
- He did not flee — he **coldly negotiated his own safe passage**:
  - Handed over his travelling companion **[[Schliegel]]** (the farmhand) and a horse to [[Melmoth]]
  - Shared **academic knowledge about the creation and care of ghouls**
- The professor then rode away **alone**, heading northeast along the [[Erengrad Road]]
- The party now knows Stradovski is not merely a rogue academic — **he is ruthless and morally capable of sacrificing others for his own survival**
