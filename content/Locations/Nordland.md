# Nordland

## Description
A northern province of The Empire, bordered by Ostland to the east and Middenland to the south. Known for its coastline on the Sea of Claws and the Laurelorn Forest. The province is ruled by a Baron and has an Ulrican religious tradition alongside ongoing tensions with neighbouring Ostland.

## Notable Features
- Border raiding between Nordland and [[Ostland]] is endemic
- The [[Laurelorn Forest]] lies within its borders, a source of political tension with Middenland
- [[Seuchenshof]] hospice is located in Nordland, treating Black Plague
