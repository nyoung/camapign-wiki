# Clerk's Office (Verena-Ulric Building)

## Description
A grand building in [[Middenheim]] with arched entryways carved with the scales of Verena and the wolf of Ulric. Smells of ink, wax, and damp parchment. Handles warrants, militia enlistment, and official legal documentation. Likely serves both the [[Temple of Verena]] and [[Cult of Ulric]] jurisdictions.

## Notable Features
- Arched entryways carved with scales of Verena and wolf of Ulric
- Equipment chest — militia gear available for deposit

## Notable NPCs
- Warrant Clerk (unnamed) — processed the party's militia warrant and oath

## Factions Present
- [[Middenland Militia]]
- [[Temple of Verena]]
- [[Cult of Ulric]]
