# Royal Wolf Inn

## Description
A fortified coaching inn and militia compound just south of the village of [[Schoten]], on the Ostland side of the Nordland border. An earthen rampart and ditches enclose a cluster of buildings — a squat stone (Kelnore) tower, a new barracks with firing slits and a flat roof with raised wall, a toll house, and the inn itself — all flying the freshly painted symbol of the [[Ostland]] bull. The barracks abuts the Kelnore tower. Soldiers drill in the courtyard and watches rotate on the walls.

The post also serves as the official Ostland border post for the Grand Principality, with a detachment of **Ostpreussiches** militia (lesser nobility and middle classes eager for plunder in the service of the state). The garrison is particularly alert because of ongoing border raiding with [[Nordland]] and because of their own recent cross-border raid.

The village of [[Schoten]] lies slightly to the north of the post, in visible decline, offering subsistence support to the garrison.

For the curious: the "wolf" in the Royal Wolf's name is not a Sigmarite image but a muted survival of an older Ostland wolf tradition (see the Cult of the Howling One). The inn sign is the Ostland bull, part of the ongoing project to erase the memories of the Ostland wolf.

## History
- **[[Session 16 - Plus ça change|Session 16]]:** Party's first overnight stop in Ostland. [[Albrecht Jylland]] confirmed two women and a brutish man (almost certainly [[Elfrieda Teuschel]]'s group) had passed through three nights prior.
- **[[Session 17 - Asper-ated|Session 17]]:** On proper arrival at the fortified post, the militia sergeant interrogated the party on suspicion of being Nordland spies and cleared them. [[Berin Garibaldi|Berin]] surrendered the Khainite tome he had been carrying into militia custody. Inside the common room, the party met [[Christina Asper]], a young Solkanite witch hunter dispatched by the University of Altdorf to assist with the pursuit of [[Professor Udo Stradovski]]. Asper confirmed [[Elfrieda Teuschel]] had stayed two nights prior, named [[Lucas Pollack]] as a second witch hunter waiting in [[Ferlangen]], and demanded (and received) [[Hildegarde]]'s Khainite-marked crossbow and black mask. [[Hildegarde]] performed for the soldiers and negotiated a two-shilling rebate on the rooms. The party departed east with Asper the next morning.
