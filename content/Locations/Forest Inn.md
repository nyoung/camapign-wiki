# Forest Inn

## Description
A heavily fortified inn on a hilltop, built to withstand a siege. Sturdy walls, shuttered windows, gate barred even in mid-afternoon. A watchtower on the northern corner holds a bell and barrels of arrows; the rocky ground prevents tunneling. Ruins of a long-abandoned human settlement surround it. Beneath its surface, a place of horror: the innkeeper [[Van Moll]] has been harbouring ghouls — transformed from starving locals during a famine two winters prior — and luring travellers to their deaths.

## Notable Features
- Heavily fortified: sturdy walls, shuttered windows, gate barred in mid-afternoon
- Watchtower on northern corner with bell and barrels of arrows
- Rocky ground prevents tunneling
- Ruins of a long-abandoned human settlement surround the inn
- No animals whatsoever — no dogs, cats, chickens, geese, cattle, or sheep (deeply wrong)
- Stew over-salted and over-spiced to gagging; kitchen locked
- Bone-filled cellar beneath the kitchen: cattle and fowl bones stripped clean (later revealed)
- All bodies from the night battle disappeared overnight — ghouls retrieved them
