# Seuchenshof

## Description
A plague hospice in Nordland, feared by locals. Also referred to as "Eichenhof" — possibly the same place, possibly an adjacent site; treat as the same or closely linked location. The road to Eichenhof has been barricaded by Skralberg militia; only plague bearers are sent that way. Those who travel toward it are not considered welcome back in civilised society. [[Van Moll]] warned it as a place of plague where no one comes back.

## Notable Features
- Road to Eichenhof barricaded by [[Skralberg]] militia
- Rumours of black plague and a Shallyan hospice
- Those who travel there are unwelcome back in civilised society
