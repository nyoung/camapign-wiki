# Skralberg

## Description
A fortified border village at the Nordland/Ostland border. Identified by blue and yellow Nordland banners. The complex includes a toll house, barracks, and a six-story watchtower. A gibbet near the road holds a body, with a raven working at its face. The town is the last Nordland settlement before entering Ostland.

## Notable Features
- Blue and yellow Nordland banners
- Toll house, barracks, and six-story watchtower
- Gibbet near the road with a body and a raven
- Road to [[Seuchenshof]] / Eichenhof barricaded by militia — only plague bearers sent that way

## Notable NPCs

- Five militiamen — intercepted the party's wagon with suspicion on arrival
- Volmar — local who accused [[Boris]] of being a Sigmarite (inn brawl)
- Mungo — [[Boris]]'s Ulrican pendant ended up in his beer mug
- Jaeger soldiers — watched the inn brawl without moving; sent over ale afterward

## Events
- **[[Session 16]] (arrival):** Five militiamen intercepted the party's wagon with suspicion, accusing them of being Ostland spies or plague bearers from Teuschenhof. [[Berin Garibaldi]] named the Drakwald Hunters (local Middenland sports team) as a credential; [[Hildegarde]] showed the Imperial warrant. They were grudgingly allowed in.
- **[[Session 16]] (inn brawl):** Volmar accused [[Boris]] of being a Sigmarite; a chair fight ensued. [[Hildegarde]] stopped it from a table. Jaeger soldiers watched without moving, then sent over ale. [[Boris]]'s Ulrican pendant ended up in Mungo's beer mug.
- **[[Session 16]] (militia sergeant intel):** [[Stradovski]] passed through approximately a week and a half before Session 16, carrying dark academic texts and a physician's bag. The road to Eichenhof was barricaded. [[Stradovski]] took the road east into Ostland. The sergeant also reported that Ostland raiders had struck a nearby farm — livestock stolen, a young farm girl abducted.
