# Collegium Theologica

## Description
A theological college in [[Middenheim]] — functions almost as a city within the city, with its own laws and security. An imposing institution. A large doorman with a polearm guards the entrance.

## Notable Features
- **Chancellor's office** — polished, smells of old parchment and sealing wax; [[Chancellor Leinhard von Altenhaus]]
- **Professor Humpis's office** — warm candlelight, perfectly aligned theological texts
- **Medical building** — includes a dissection theatre; [[Professor Udo Stradovski]]'s office is here
  - Stradovski's office: cluttered papers, stacks of books, vials, medical instruments, chemical odours and dry herbs; secret compartment in desk drawer
- **Prison cells** — surprisingly luxurious: feather mattresses, desks, books, scent of lavender; far superior to typical Imperial jails; [[Augustus Steiner]] is held here

## Notable NPCs
- [[Chancellor Leinhard von Altenhaus]] — Chancellor
- [[Professor Humpis]] — professor of law; original accuser of Stradovski
- [[Stellman (Student Guide)]] — student assigned to escort the party
- [[Watch Captain Tringen Melbur]] — oversees the cells
- [[Augustus Steiner]] — imprisoned in the cells

## Factions Present
- [[Ordo Sancti Inquisita]] — uses the cells

## Connected Locations

## History
- [[Professor Udo Stradovski]] worked here for over twenty years as a medical professor before fleeing

## Notes
- Dissection theatre in the medical building — students observe cadavers being worked on
- The cells are more comfortable than expected; may reflect the institution's power and autonomy
