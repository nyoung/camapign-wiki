# Erengrad Road

## Description
The main road north through Nordland, running from [[Beekerhoven]] northward through forests, rocky scrubland, and eventually to the [[Skralberg]] border post and into [[Ostland]]. Named for the distant northern city of Erengrad. The road passes through increasingly sparse and hostile terrain — thinning forest, poor sandy soil, fallow farms, and scattered inns fortified against beastman and mutant attack.

## Notable Features
- Landscape shifts dramatically: dense forest → rocky scrubland with nutrient-poor soil → canyon-like open land
- Farms along this stretch have almost entirely switched to sheep for wool rather than crops or cattle
- Road wardens patrol regularly and bring mutant bodies through for destruction
- Toll gates at intervals — some bypassed by [[Elfrieda Teuschel]]'s group via sheep pasture fields

## Notes
- The road is described as rough and dangerous this early in the season
- Mutant activity has been increasing; travelers have gone missing between inns for years
- After Skralberg, the road continues into Ostland
