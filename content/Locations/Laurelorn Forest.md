# Laurelorn Forest

## Description
A forest to the west of the Nordland road, home to Wood Elves. Tensions between the Wood Elves of the Laurelorn and the humans of Nordland have been rising, driven by the Baron of Nordland's aggressive posturing toward the elves' territory. The elves have retreated and severed contact, causing the trade that once flowed through places like the [[Temple to Liadriel]] to dry up entirely.

## Notable Features
- Home to Wood Elves
- Elves have withdrawn from contact with humans following the Baron of Nordland's aggression
