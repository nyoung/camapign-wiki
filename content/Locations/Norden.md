# Norden

## Description
A destination north of the [[Hemp Merchant]], reached via the well-kept track running north from the coast road. The party has not visited. The **Imperialist Engineer Pioneer Corps** has been ordered to upgrade the entire road and bridges between the [[Hemp Merchant]] and Norden to support the **heaviest wagons**, working day and night from both ends of the route.

## Notable Features
- Terminus of a major infrastructure effort in progress under direct Imperial orders

## Connected Locations

- Referenced in [[Session 18 - The Hemp Merchant|Session 18]] from military conversations at the Hemp Merchant: destination of the road-and-bridge upgrade; blueprints on the officers' table all pointed north
