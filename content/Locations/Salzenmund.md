# Salzenmund

## Description
A city north of [[Beekerhoven]], in Nordland. The Inquisitors (Sigmarite and Ulrican knights) departed Beekerhoven on the morning of 22 Nachexen 2512 heading here, expecting to arrive in approximately two days (24 Nachexen). Likely [[Professor Udo Stradovski]]'s next destination, as the Inquisitors are following his trail.

## Connected Locations
- [[Beekerhoven]] — south

## Notes
- The Inquisitors are approximately two days ahead of the party (as of 23 Nachexen 2512)
- Party plans to pursue via the Beekerhoven–Salzenmund road
- Forest roads between the two cities are described as dangerous at night due to highwaymen
