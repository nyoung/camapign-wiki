# Middenheim

## Description
A large, imposing city. Built atop a massive rock — the Fauschlag — with sheer cliff walls. Known for its towering city walls. A blustery, cold place. Strongly associated with the Cult of Ulric; the Ulrican/Sigmarite religious tension is a live political issue.

## Notable Features
- Massive imposing city walls
- Deep ditch running along the wall — site of the sewer attack in [[Session 01 - The Sewer Horror]]
- Sewer infrastructure that appears to be in disrepair
- A plaza near the [[Worm Lamplight]]
