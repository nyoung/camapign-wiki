# Arenburg

## Description
A run-down village on the road north from [[Middenheim]] toward [[Beekerhoven]]. Muddy streets, ramshackle buildings, acrid smell of waste and smoke.

## Notable Features
- **Four Seasons inn** — the first coaching inn; was full when the caravan arrived; redirected overflow to the Wolfrunner establishment
- **Wolfrunner inn** — also full
- **[[The Raised Mug]]** — a third, struggling inn where the party stayed; tilted sign, sagging shutters

## Notable NPCs
- [[Gunter]] — local; was treated by [[Professor Udo Stradovski]] ten days prior
- Innkeeper at the Four Seasons (unnamed) — redirected parties
- Innkeeper at [[The Raised Mug]] (unnamed, thin and worried) — eager for business

## Connected Locations
- [[Middenheim]] — south
- [[Beekerhoven]] — north

## Notes
- [[Professor Udo Stradovski]] stayed here ~10 days before the party's visit; treated [[Gunter]]'s knee gratis
- The Raised Mug's proprietor is losing business to coaching company inns — potential information source
