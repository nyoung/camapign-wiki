# The Raised Mug

## Description
A struggling inn in [[Arenburg]]. Tilted sign, sagging shutters. Run by a thin, worried innkeeper who welcomed the party eagerly — large coaching lines have built their own inns and now direct traffic away from independent establishments like his.
