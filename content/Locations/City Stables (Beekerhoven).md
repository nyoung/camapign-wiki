# City Stables (Beekerhoven)

## Description
The only remaining functioning stable in [[Beekerhoven]] after the inn's stable burned down. A practical, working stable that serves travellers and locals alike. Owner [[Castor Stark]] charges 4 pennies per night for boarding.

## Notable Features
- Only functioning stable left in [[Beekerhoven]] following the fire at the inn's stable
- Boards horses at 4 pennies per night

## Notable NPCs
- [[Castor Stark]] — owner
- Stable boy — a valuable informant; knows the comings and goings of travellers and their mounts

## Connected Locations
- [[Beekerhoven]] — parent settlement

## Events
- **[[Session 09 - The Thriceling|Session 09]]:** Party brought [[Orazio]] here for boarding. The stable boy proved a valuable informant — reported that [[Professor Udo Stradovski|Stradovski]] had bought an old nag and claimed he was heading to [[Salzenmund]]; also confirmed that [[Marx Khlesl]] had returned riding the professor's horse. [[Berin Garibaldi]] stayed the night here watching over [[Orazio]].
- **[[Session 11 - Rolf's Rampage and the Writ of Arrest|Session 11]]:** Party returned with [[Hoffman Jannitzer]] to press the stable boy further. He revealed that [[Marx Khlesl]] had been at the [[Medical Union Compound]].

## Notes
- The stable boy's informal intelligence on horse traffic made him an unexpectedly useful contact during the Beekerhoven investigation
