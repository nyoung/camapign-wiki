# Ferlangen

## Description
A mining town in eastern Ostland. [[Professor Stradovski]]'s confirmed destination, established by the note found by [[Falconius]] in the [[Medical Union Compound]] and signed by [[E. Teuschel]]. The party has been pursuing [[Stradovski]] toward this location since [[Session 11]].

## Notable Features
- Mining town in eastern Ostland
- Approximately 10–12 days from [[Beekerhoven]] by the northern coastal road
- Approximately 2.5–3 weeks by the safer southern route around the Grey Mountains

## Connected Locations

- [[Schuten]] — first Ostland town east of the border

## Events
- **[[Session 11]]:** [[Falconius]] found a note in the [[Medical Union Compound]] signed by [[E. Teuschel]], confirming [[Stradovski]] was heading to Ferlangen. This established the party's destination.
- **[[Session 13]]:** [[Condradine]] claimed to be travelling from Ferlangen to [[Middenheim]].
- **[[Session 16]]:** [[Stradovski]] confirmed to have passed through [[Skralberg]] heading east toward Ostland and Ferlangen.
