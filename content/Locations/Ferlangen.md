# Ferlangen

## Description
A mining town in eastern Ostland. [[Professor Udo Stradovski|Professor Stradovski]]'s confirmed destination, established by the note found by [[Falconius Thorncobble|Falconius]] in the [[Medical Union Compound]] and signed by [[Elfrieda Teuschel|E. Teuschel]]. The party has been pursuing [[Professor Udo Stradovski|Stradovski]] toward this location since [[Session 11 - Rolf's Rampage and the Writ of Arrest|Session 11]].

## Notable Features
- Mining town in eastern Ostland
- Approximately 10–12 days from [[Beekerhoven]] by the northern coastal road
- Approximately 2.5–3 weeks by the safer southern route around the Grey Mountains
