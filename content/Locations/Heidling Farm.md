# Heidling Farm

## Description
A farmstead roughly fifteen miles north of the [[Pewter Merchant]]. The party has not visited. Per [[Herga]], the Heidling family is known for crossing the Nordland border to steal **livestock and people alike**.

## Notable NPCs
- **[[Gustavus]]** — raider leader suspected to operate out of the Heidling farm; killed the impostor at the [[Pewter Merchant]]

## Connected Locations
- [[Pewter Merchant]] — roughly fifteen miles south
- Nordland — across the border, source of the family's raiding targets

## History
- Mentioned by [[Herga]] in [[Session 18 - The Hemp Merchant|Session 18]] as the likely base of the raiders who kidnapped her and were bringing her to be sold at the [[Pewter Merchant]]

## Notes
- If the party intends to pursue [[Gustavus]] and the raiders, this is where to look
