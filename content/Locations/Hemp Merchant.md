# Hemp Merchant

## Description
A medium-size coaching inn at the junction where the [[Ostland]] coast road meets a well-kept track running north into the forest. Its sign shows a coil of rope over an anchor. When the party arrived in [[Session 18 - The Hemp Merchant|Session 18]], the compound was swarming with **Imperialist Engineer Pioneer Corps** soldiers and [[Landsturm]] militia, with supply wagons rumbling in and out of the northern track even as dusk settled.

## Notable Features
- Stabling — overcrowded in Session 18, requiring [[Berin Garibaldi|Berin]] to improvise space for seven horses and a wagon
- A great room heavy with soldiers and officers
- Blueprints for **bridges and road sections**, all pointing north, on the officers' table
- Located at the coast-road/northern-track junction

## Notable NPCs
- [[Jan Smuts]] — proprietor; overwhelmed but cooperative; identified the [[Pewter Merchant]] corpse as his missing servant boy
- Terrified stable boy — confirmed [[Professor Udo Stradovski|Stradovski]]'s stay on the 16th Nachexen
- **Mentioned:** [[Albrecht Felt]] — a merchant who stayed here and vanished with the servant boy three or four days prior
- Unnamed senior enlisted men of the Imperialist Engineer Pioneer Corps — talked to Pilpoul
- Unnamed officers — blueprints on their table

## Factions Present
- **Imperialist Engineer Pioneer Corps** — upgrading the road and bridges to [[Norden]] under direct Imperial orders
- [[Landsturm]] militia — called up and stationed here; insignia mean a year of lost taxes per man

## Connected Locations
- [[Pewter Merchant]] — west along the coast road
- [[Norden]] — north, along the well-kept track being upgraded
- [[Ferlangen]] — east along the coast road, the party's destination

## History
- **[[Session 18 - The Hemp Merchant|Session 18]]:** Party arrived at dusk with [[Christina Asper]] and [[Herga]]; found the compound under heavy military use. [[Jan Smuts]] confirmed passage of both [[Professor Udo Stradovski|Stradovski]] (16th Nachexen) and [[Elfrieda Teuschel]]'s group (two nights prior, paying with a freshly minted crown). When [[Boris Dorchen|Boris]] described the Pewter Merchant corpse's birthmark, Smuts identified the body as his own servant boy, vanished three or four days ago with the merchant [[Albrecht Felt]]. [[Pilpoul Ivri|Pilpoul]] glimpsed road-and-bridge blueprints pointing north and learned from enlisted men that the corps had been ordered to upgrade the road to Norden to support the heaviest wagons, working day and night from both ends.

## Notes
- The scale of the military operation is disproportionate to a simple road upgrade — direct Imperial orders, Landsturm with expensive tax concessions, artisans and engineers called from across the Empire
- The servant boy's identification effectively solves the Pewter Merchant murder mystery — the "noble" was never a noble
- Stradovski's trail confirmed here; still heading east
- Elfrieda's lead over the party has closed to ~2 days
